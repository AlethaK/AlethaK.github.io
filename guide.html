GUIDELINES!!!

This would be the first topic to learn in the genre of Singing. PHONATION is the act of producing vocal sound in either speechin or singing. it involves the vocal folds( also called vocal chords).

Relaxation of the tongue and throat are crucial in allowing your "best" voice.

Three actions cause the vocal folds to close:

1. Swallowing : muscles engaging when you over-articulate the words.

2. Bearing Down : lifting heavy ojects, in which vocal folds close in order to build thoracic pressure and provide strength.

3. Producing sound : levels of tension to produce the different pitches on which we sing or speak.

Nerves impulses originating in the abdominal area help the vocal folds to close properly for singing or speech. Thus, the sound must start with the breathe from he abdominal area.

the process id fourfold:

1. Inhalation, or breathing in

2. Suspension

3. Exhalation, or breathing out

4. Recovery
